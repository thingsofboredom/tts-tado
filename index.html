Selecting a voice

You can select a voice by adding VoiceName:. Replace VoiceName with the actual name of the voice and keep the colon. Example:

This text uses the default voice. Matthew: This text uses the Matthew voice. Ivy: And this text uses the Ivy voice.

Sounds

You can also add sounds to your tts by adding the sound name in parentheses (SoundName). Example:

Your funny message (fart)

In this example Your funny message is getting read by the tts and then the sound (fart) is played.
